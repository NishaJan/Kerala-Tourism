KOTTAYAM-TRAVEL GUIDE

Kottayam is a land of unique characteristics. Panoramic backwater stretches, lush paddy fields, highlands, hills and hillocks, extensive rubber plantations, places associated with many legends and a totally literate people have given Kottayam District the enviable title: The land of letters, legends, latex and lakes.

How to Reach Kottayam:

By road:An excellent network of roads connects the district to all the major towns and cities of India.

By rail:You can easily get regular trains to Kottayam from other major cities of the country. Railway Station Kottayam.

By air: Nearest airport is Cochin International Airport, Ernakulam District (90 Km).

Places to visit in Kottayam:
  1. Malarikkal Village Tourism

    2. These are the famous water lilies of Malarikkal, a rising tourist hotbed in Kerala, located in the southern tip of India. This natural phenomenon in the paddy fields turns the entire landscape into a luscious pink carpet that is a mesmerizing sight to behold.

  2. Vemabanad Lake

    3. Kottayam has a vast network of rivers and canals, which empty into the great expanse of water called the Vembanad Lake.In the Vembanad Lake there is a small beautiful island called Pathiramanal or the Midnight sands. This lonely island in the Vemabanad Lake is accessible only by boat.

  3. Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary

    4. An attraction of Kumarakom is the Bird sanctuary spreading over 14 acres of land. Located on the banks of the Vembanad Lake, the sanctuary is a favourite haunt of migratory birds like the Siberian Stork, egret, darter, heron and teal. Local birds like the water fowl, cuckoo, owl and water hen, other common varieties like the wood pecker, sky lark, crane and parrots can also be spotted here.

  4. Vagamon

    5. Situated 64kms from Kottayam, this enchanting hill station at an elevation of 1100 metres above sea level is a trekker’s paradise.
